prpject2:

METRO DATE DATING APP UX DESIGN

Metro Date is a mobile dating app, targeting users who are young and commute by metro in New York City. Metro Date is a platform designed for the youth in NYC to share their interesting encounters in subway station and meet the potential significant others. It provides a new matching algorithm, matching by similarity of commuting routes and a set of ice breaking strategies.
